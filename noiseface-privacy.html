NoiseFace Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 24, 2025

Overview

NoiseFace is committed to protecting your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Information We Don't Collect

NoiseFace does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device information
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Location data
  • Audio recordings
  • Any other data about you or your device

How NoiseFace Works

All audio generation and processing happens entirely on your device. NoiseFace:

  • Generates audio waveforms locally on your device
  • Saves your preset preferences locally using iOS standard storage
  • Does not connect to the internet
  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not share any information with third parties

Data Storage

The only data stored by NoiseFace is:

  • Your saved preset configurations (which noises are active and their settings)
  • This data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults
  • This data never leaves your device
  • You can delete this data by deleting the app

Third-Party Services

NoiseFace does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Because NoiseFace does not collect any information, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. However, since we don't collect any data, changes are unlikely.
