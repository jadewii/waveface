About WaveFace

WaveFace is a powerful waveform generator app that lets you create and customize audio waveforms. Choose from 20+ different waveforms including sine, square, sawtooth, triangle, and many more.

Getting Started

When you first launch WaveFace, you'll have access to 4 free waveforms:

  • Sine
  • Square
  • Sawtooth
  • Triangle

Tap any waveform to start playing it. Use the controls to adjust frequency, volume, and other parameters.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock all waveforms?

Tap any locked waveform to see the purchase option. For a one-time payment of $4.99, you'll unlock all 20+ premium waveforms permanently.

How do I restore my purchase?

If you've already purchased the full version but don't see your premium waveforms unlocked:

  1. Tap any locked waveform
  2. In the upgrade dialog, tap "Restore"
  3. Your purchase will be restored from the App Store

Can I use WaveFace on multiple devices?

Yes! Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID and will sync across all your iOS devices automatically.

How do I change the waveform settings?

While playing a waveform, use the on-screen controls to adjust:

  • Frequency (pitch)
  • Volume
  • Visual style

The app isn't making any sound. What should I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check that your device volume is turned up
  2. Make sure your device isn't in silent mode
  3. Check the in-app volume slider
  4. Try closing and reopening the app
  5. Restart your device if the issue persists
